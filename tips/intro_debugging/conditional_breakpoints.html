Conditional Breakpoints¶

Debugging with breakpoints is very useful, but sometimes you have to click continue many times to get to the item in a loop that you want to break on. In a repetitive edit-debug-edit cycle, this can get frustrating.

Instead, attach a condition on a breakpoint, making it only stop when the condition is met. The condition has access to variables in scope at the breakpoint.
