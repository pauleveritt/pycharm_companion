topic: Scratches

Scratches¶

Sometimes you just have a snippet of code you’d like to toy with. Or some notes from a phone call. Nothing you want to commit to saving in your project, and certainly nothing to put in VCS.

PyCharm scratches provide this. You can use a scratch buffer (unformatted text) or a scratch file (the extension dictates the language.) These are stored apart from the sources of your project.
